Our Work Flow
The thing that makes anyone successful is the ability to tackle work in the correct order.
Tekolio tackles the work by dividing it and dealing with it one at a time that also includes the client-side of dealing.
Step-I - Contact
Many people are afraid of sharing their ideas with companies or are just afraid to take the first step. Tekolio offers you many platforms to come and speak your mind, and it's up to us to make it happen.
We are here to turn your imagination into reality.
Step-II - Meeting Time
Discussion is one of the most crucial steps as through this you can exactly tell your requirements and will work according to them.
Step-III - Content Collection
We are very specific with the content as it is one of the key factors that will describe the customers about the website, business and type of work being offered. We will collect any type of data that you have and some wxtra through our discussion and with the help of both, the website will be created.
Step-IV - Analysis And Planning
At this point, your work is over, and its time for our technical team to handle the rest of the work. According to the data collected, a strategy will be made for each section having its deadline.
Step-V - Design And Development
There are different departments for both Development and Designing so that the work can be completed in time. Now it will be checked by both developers and designers before it goes for testing to our quality and responsiveness tests.
Step-VI - Quality Check
This is our first and last testing area as we are confident that any type of mistake will be identified and then rectified as we want to give our clients our best work we can present.
Step-VII - Review Time
In this step client sees the site and reviews it and if any change or addition is required, it can easily be done as the website is still in its development phase.
Step-VIII - Revision Time
Now its our turn to go through the website once more after the required changes before giving the final product to the client.
Step-IX - Project Delivery
Its time to give the client his website which has cleared all our tests and now is ready to see the outside world and start working in place of you.
